scream24

Scream24 (Scream revision 2024) is a programming language completely written in incoherent screams. It includes such incredible instructions
as "AAEEeuueoeoaEAEOeueAEOAEOAEeueAOEOEAeueuaEOeOEUEUAEeoaoeUAEEA". I've featured scream24 in my blog post
